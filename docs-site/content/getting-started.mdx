# Getting Started

ClearSight scans websites for WCAG 2.1 Level A and AA accessibility violations, enriches findings with AI, and delivers actionable reports.

## Prerequisites

- A running ClearSight instance (see [deployment docs](/deployment))
- A website URL to scan

## Your First Scan

### Quick Single-Page Scan

1. Open the **Dashboard**
2. Enter a URL in the scan form (e.g., `https://example.com`)
3. Click **Scan**
4. Wait for the pipeline to complete (~30-60 seconds per page)
5. Review your results

### Full Site Crawl

1. Open the **Dashboard**
2. Enter your site URL — ClearSight will create a Site record
3. Navigate to the Site Overview page
4. Click **Crawl Now**
5. Watch the live progress as pages are discovered and scanned
6. Review aggregated results across all pages

## Understanding Results

After a scan completes, you'll see:

- **Score** — 0-100 accessibility score
- **Issues** — categorized as Confirmed or Potential
- **Severity** — Critical, Serious, Moderate, or Minor
- **AI Summary** — overview of your page's accessibility
- **Fix Suggestions** — actionable steps for each issue
- **Visual Inspector** — see the exact element on a screenshot
